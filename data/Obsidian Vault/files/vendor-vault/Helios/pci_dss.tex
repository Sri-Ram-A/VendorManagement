\section*{2. Section 2: Report on Compliance Summary}
\VendorName\ confirms that its \SystemName\ has been assessed against the requirements of
PCI-DSS v\PCIVersion. The assessment covered all applicable requirements for a service
provider handling cardholder data.

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=2em]
  \item \textbf{Data Store Context:} Full Primary Account Numbers (PANs) and Sensitive
  Authentication Data (SAD/CVV) are stored only in an encrypted state post-authorization,
  using industry-standard \PCIEncryptionStandard\ encryption with secure key management
  practices.
  \item Scope of assessment includes network segmentation, cardholder data environments
  (CDE), and all systems involved in payment processing, authorization, and settlement.
\end{itemize}
