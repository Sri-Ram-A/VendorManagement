\vspace{1em}
To the Management of \VendorName\ and the Users of \VendorName's \SystemName:

We have examined management's assertion that \VendorName\ (the ``Service Organization'')
maintained effective controls over its \SystemName\ (the ``System'') based on the Trust
Services Criteria for Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality, and
Privacy (the ``Applicable Trust Services Criteria'') for the period \AuditPeriodStart\ to
\AuditPeriodEnd. Management is responsible for its assertion and the design, implementation,
and operation of the controls. Our responsibility is to express an opinion on the fairness of
the presentation of management's description and the suitability of the design and operating
effectiveness of the controls.

\section*{1. Scope}
The System consists of the infrastructure, software, people, procedures, and data necessary
to provide payment authorization, settlement, and transaction routing services to clients
such as \ClientName.

\section*{2. Opinion}
In our opinion, except for the matter described in the Trust Services Criteria Exceptions
section of this report, the controls stated were fairly presented and operated with
effective efficiency throughout the review period.

\section*{3. Management's Description of the System}
[Management's description of the system, policies, and procedures has been included in the
full report and is fairly presented in all material respects.]

\section*{4. Testing of Operating Effectiveness}
We performed tests of the operating effectiveness of controls over the Applicable Trust
Services Criteria. The nature, timing, and extent of testing performed are described in the
accompanying Test of Controls matrix.

\section*{5. Trust Services Criteria Exceptions}
The following exceptions were noted during our testing:

\begin{longtable}{p{3.2cm} p{2.2cm} p{8cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Criteria} & \textbf{Exception ID} & \textbf{Description} \\
\midrule
\endhead
\ExceptionCriteria &
\ExceptionID &
Testing of a random sample of \ExceptionSampleSize\ production servers revealed that
automated cryptographic key rotation routines failed to fire at the required
\ExceptionThresholdDays-day intervals. \ExceptionAffectedCount\ core database instances were
running keys older than \ExceptionActualDays\ days due to \ExceptionRootCause. \\
\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

This exception represents a deviation from the control objectives related to cryptographic
key management and secure data protection practices within the Core Production Infrastructure.

\section*{6. Management's Response}
\VendorName\ management acknowledges the identified exception related to cryptographic key
rotation. A manual remediation process was implemented immediately upon notification. Full
system compliance with automated key rotation controls was restored on \RemediationDate.
Management has enhanced monitoring and alerting mechanisms to prevent recurrence and will
conduct a comprehensive review of all related system scripts.

\section*{7. Other Information}
This report is intended solely for the information and use of \VendorName\ management, its
clients (including but not limited to \ClientName), and other specified parties who have a
business relationship with \VendorName\ and who are subject to the Applicable Trust Services
Criteria. This report is not intended for any other purpose.

The report, including any exceptions noted, does not provide any assurance regarding future
periods. Controls are subject to inherent limitations, including the possibility of
management override.
